Weeknight Pizza

Who knew the best pizza in town could be so simple?

Method

Pizza oven

Ingredients

Publix Bakery fresh pizza dough, flour, pizza sauce, mozzarella cheese, pepperonis, 1 jalapeno, Mike's Hot Honey

Steps

One hour before you plan to begin, remove pizza dough from fridge

Preheat pizza oven on high heat. On ours, this is screaming hot, around 700 degrees

Slice pepperonis and jalapenos. Add light dusting of flour to work surface

Separate pizza dough into three dough balls

Stretch each dough ball by hand and flatten it using a rolling pin until desired thickness is reached (we flatten until it's the width of our pizza peel)

Next, spread a light amount of pizza sauce, then add mozzarella cheese, pepperonis, and jalapenos to each

Cook on high until desired doneness (1-2 minutes), then remove from pizza oven and let rest 5 minutes

Top with Mike's Hot Honey, then slice and enjoy
