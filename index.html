Born from Memphis barbecue roots and built in Huntsville, Alabama, Rocket City Rubs is all about fire, flavor, and connection. Founder Matt Edwards turned a lifelong love of barbecue into a hands-on craft after moving to Huntsville in 2021. Inspired by the people gathered around the grill, he believes great barbecue isn’t just cooked—it’s shared. Our seasoning blends help turn everyday grilling into memorable moments around the table.
