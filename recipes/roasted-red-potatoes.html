Roasted Red Potatoes

Simple side that goes great with any entree

Method

Pellet grill or oven

Ingredients

Roasted red potatoes, olive oil, your favorite seasoning combo

  • Salt, pepper, and dry ranch seasoning
  • Meat Church Blanco

Steps

Wash red potatoes and cut into bite sized pieces

In a large bowl, add sliced potatoes, olive oil and seasoning, then mix well

Preheat grill or oven to 400 degrees F

Cook red potatoes to desired level of doneness (we like crispy which is close to 45 minutes)

Serve up plain or with a side of ranch for dipping
