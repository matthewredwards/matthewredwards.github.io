Chicken Thighs

First cook on the new pellet grill

Method

Pellet grill

Ingredients

Boneless skinless chicken thighs, your favorite marinade and seasoning combo

  • Lawrys Honey Bourbon marinade + BBQ seasoning
  • Kraft Zesty Italian dressing + Greek seasoning

Steps

Marinate chicken thighs for 4-6 hours

Remove from marinade, then apply a moderate layer of seasoning

Preheat grill to 400 degrees F

Cook chicken thighs to internal temp of 195 to 205 degrees F (40-45 minutes)

Serve up and enjoy. Goes well with Fresh Express Bacon Thousand Island chopped salad kit
