Reverse Sear Steak

A perfect balance of smoke and sear

Method

Pellet grill + gas grill

Ingredients

One porterhouse, olive oil, your favorite steak seasoning (we used Ben's Heffer Dust), garlic herb butter

Steps

Preheat pellet grill to 200 degrees F

Lightly coat steak with olive oil, then apply a light to moderate layer of seasoning

Smoke steak until it reaches an internal temp of 125 degrees F (1-1.5 hours)

Remove and preheat gas grill to high heat while steaks rest

Sear 2-3 minutes per side or until it reaches target internal temp (around 140 degrees F for medium)

Remove, top with garlic herb butter, and let rest 5 minutes

Pair with salad or brown rice and enjoy

Inspired by Recteq Reverse Seared Ribeye with Herb Butter recipe
