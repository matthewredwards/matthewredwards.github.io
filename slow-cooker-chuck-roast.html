0 to 400 Wings

Crispy, crunchy and flavorful, this wing recipe is a crowd pleaser

Method

Pellet grill

Ingredients

Chicken wings, your favorite BBQ rub and sauce combo

  • Chef Dennis' Grilled Dry Rub Chicken Wing rub + Meat Mitch's Yellow Brick Road sauce
  • Meat Church The Gospel rub + Sweet Baby Ray's Buffalo Wing sauce

Steps

Rinse and dry chicken wings, then season generously on both sides and allow to adhere

Place seasoned wings on wire oven rack for easy transport

Put wire rack in pellet grill, then start grill and set temp to 400 degrees F

Cook wings until internal temp reaches 200 to 205 degrees F

Remove wings from grill

Place wings in large bowl and add desired amount of sauce, then toss thoroughly

Put wings back on grill for 5 minutes to tack up sauce

Remove from grill, serve up with a side of ranch and enjoy
